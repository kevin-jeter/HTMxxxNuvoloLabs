Lab 1: Orientation and Navigation

Part 1: Creating Favorites

Many, if not all of the features you will be using will be located under the Clinical Asset Management and Clinical Asset Management Admin modules. For ease of navigation, we will show you how to favorite and pin those lists, so you always have easy access.

  1. Login to Nuvolo using: vaoittest.servicenowservices.com.
  2. When prompted, select your VA.gov account to sign in. (Image placeholder)
  3. Upon login, confirm you see the banner at the top of your screen. (Image placeholder)
  4. At the top left corner of your browser, select All. (Image placeholder)
  5. In the dropdown list, scroll until you see Clinical Asset Management and Clinical Asset Management Admin. (Image placeholder)
  6. You can also type “Clinical Asset Management” into your search bar.
  7. Hover your mouse over Clinical Asset Management.
  8. Click the star icon to the right of Clinical Asset Management. (Image placeholder)
  9. Confirm your Favorites box matches the screenshot, then select Done. (Image placeholder)
  10. Repeat steps 6–9 for Clinical Asset Management Admin.
  11. Select Favorites at the top left corner of your browser and pin your Favorites bar. (Image placeholder)

Part 2: Personalizing your Favorites

There are over 100 modules and selectable options under Clinical Asset Management and Clinical Asset Management Admin. While they all serve a specific purpose, some will be much more beneficial than others. Remove menus, lists, and options that you will not use to streamline your UI. You can also edit your Favorites bar to make it more personalized and easier to use.

  1. Reference your Favorites bar and expand Clinical Asset Management. (Image placeholder)
  2. Scroll to Emergency Events & Contacts, hover, and click the X to remove it. Confirm removal. (Image placeholder)
  3. The module will be removed from Favorites. You can still access it from the All menu. Take a minute to remove other modules you don’t use frequently.
  4. Click the pencil/notepad icon to edit favorites. Explore options to change colors, order, and icons. Be sure to click Save Edits before leaving. (Image placeholder)

Part 3: Navigating Dashboards

Dashboards are a key feature within Nuvolo. Add dashboards to Favorites, familiarize yourself with the dashboard page, and learn how to create and access shared dashboards.

  1. Select All and hover over Dashboards. Click the star icon to favorite. (Image placeholder)
  2. Confirm the Favorites box and select Done. (Image placeholder)
  3. Select Dashboards within your pinned Favorites bar. (Image placeholder)
  4. The following image is a layout and familiarization of the dashboard page (labels a–e). (Image placeholder)
  • Create a Dashboard: Create and name your dashboard; assign roles for members.
  • Convenience Tabs: View dashboards by Recent, Owned by Me, Shared with Me, and All.
  • Group Filter: Filter by groups such as HTM Dashboards and Software Asset Management.
  • Search Dashboard: Search for a specific dashboard.
  • Dashboard Tile: Each dashboard appears as a tile in the grid—click to open.

Part 7: Scavenger Hunt

Use your profile, preferences, dashboards, Favorites bar, and Clinical Asset Management to explore Nuvolo and answer the following:

  1. How would you find the dashboard titled “HTM – My Open PM List”?
  2. Who owns the dashboard titled “HTM – My Open PM List”?
  3. What are the 4 available options under the Safety & Recall Alerts list?
  4. On what tab can you see upcoming and assigned PMs?
  5. What is the number value for Overall Working Capacity of Facility Name: HTM, Description: HTM Program Office? (Hint: look for “organizations and spaces”)
  6. What are the first 5 value columns within My Work Orders?
  7. Where can you go to change your time zone?
  8. Where can you see a list of all dashboards?
  9. Under Favorites, where does Home take you?
  10. Change one of your Favorites to the color purple.
